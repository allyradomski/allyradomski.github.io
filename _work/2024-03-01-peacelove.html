--- title: Peacelove. description: About feeling present and connected. cover: /assets/posts/2024/peacelove/cover.jpg eventtitle: '"iridescence" trans* care exhibition & Instructions for Curating a Transnational Exhibition' startdate: March 2024 & February 2025 tags: - Processing - Writing - Photography --- I gathered writings and photographs, most of which are from places where I felt entirely present and connected with the world.
I found beauty while being alone. I remember the times so vividly.
The work is displayed on a CRT and consists of writings being etched on the screen and photos changing in the background. Generative sound accompanies with field recordings and etching noises.

The diplay is dressed in fabrics that remind me of home and patterns sewed onto the front piece. Rocks further decorate.
