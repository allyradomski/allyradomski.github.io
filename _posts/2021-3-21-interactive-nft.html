--- title: Interactive web NFTs description: Possibly a few of the first interactive music NFTs. media: /assets/sketches/2021-21-3-interactive-nft tags: - JavaScript --- During the somewhat early days of NFTs, I signed up to a platform called Hic Et Nunc. This platform was based on cryptocurrency Tezos, a self-proclaimed clean cryptocurrency. The site allowed you to upload music files, image files and SVG files. Someone discovered you could have interactive elements through SVG files on the site which opened it up to generative and interactive NFTs.
