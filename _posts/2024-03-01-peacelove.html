# Feeling entirely present and utterly connected to the world. #

# Seeing individuality in the everyday, a great appreciation. #

# An imperturbable peace and love for the current. #

Peacelove.

I gathered writings and photographs, most of which are from places where I felt entirely present and connected with the world.
I found beauty while being alone. I remember the times so vividly.
The work is displayed on a CRT and consists of writings being etched on the screen and photos changing in the background. Generative sound accompanies with field recordings and etching noises.

The diplay is dressed in fabrics that remind me of home and patterns sewed onto the front piece. Rocks further decorate.
