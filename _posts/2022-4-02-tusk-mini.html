TUSK Mini @ Star and Shadow

The real-time installation displays manipulated satellite images of Newcastle (location of event). The satellite image is chosen randomly as it happens. The rumbling and grainy audio paired with the installation is designed to put the viewers into a trance-like state. The installation ran constantly on both days of the event.
